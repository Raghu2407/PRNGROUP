Management

  • Management

The company is managed by highly qualified and dynamic individuals with decades of experience in the construction and engineering space.

The partners of the firm have good focus on the deliverables and are well acquainted with the complexities of infrastructure projects and provide the required direction needed to the highly qualified CEO who has over 25+ years’ experience in the infrastructure domain.

USP OF THE COMPANY

  • Quality
  • Timely Completion
  • Safety & Health
  • Environmentally Sustainable
  • Own Supply Chain
  • Good HR Practices
  • Optimal Execution
  • Strong Geographic Reach

Quality: The Company is obsessed with the quality of deliverables and is well known for following stringent quality management systems throughout its supply chain ensuring end deliverables are of the highest quality.

Timely Completion: The Company has developed internal processes that ensure efficiency in execution and ensure timely completion of projects, because most of the equipment and materials supply chain are also of its own, this eliminates third-party risk and delays enabling quick execution of projects.

Safety & Health: The Company has taken safety initiatives by training the staff in safety and material handling apart from providing safety gears and equipment’s. The company provides own accommodation and subsidised hygienic meals at all its construction sites for all its employees.

Environmentally Sustainable: The company ensures that all the designs and subsequent activities related to construction are environmentally sustainable and due attention is given by management to ensure all waste is disposed of appropriately without causing any environmental damage..

Own Supply Chain: The Company has its own end-to-end infrastructure & machinery along with in-house maintenance processes leading to negligible supply chain disruptions and equipment down-times.

Good HR Practices: The HR Practices lay emphasis on fair treatment to all employees, a good career path with plenty of opportunities for learning new technology and modern methods of functioning, above industry standard remuneration to personnel ensuring very low employee turnover. Most of the top officials are with the company since its inception which is a testimony to our ‘we care for our employees’ HR policy.

Optimal Execution: The Company has a record of executing all its projects in time and within the customer’s budget seldom leading to delays and over-budget scenarios except in force-majeure cases.

Strong Geographic Reach: The Company today has extensive geographic reach with the addition of many branch offices across various locations to facilitate liaising with customers and the government authorities..
